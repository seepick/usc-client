Change with Pilates

4.5
(31)
Yoga








Do you want to love the Pilates method — without pressure? Do you want to experience BASI-Style postural improvement that’s gradual, sustainable, and effective? To the ClassPass community: We invite you to our Boutique Mat Pilates Classes — with a maximum of 5 people. Body Weight Classes with the added challenge of resistance and weights. Expect a full-body workout that builds strength, flexibility, and stamina. Unlike crowded studios, here you’re truly seen and supported — with personal attention, real corrections, and a welcoming atmosphere. In our fully BASI Systems Equipped Pilates Studio, we also offer: -Private and Duet Reformer Sessions - Specialized Training for Scoliosis, Pregnancy, Pelvic Floor & Osteoporosis. -Athletic Conditioning with Pilates & Weight Training (with Pulley-System like in the Gyms). Pilates is not punishment. It’s a powerful, healthy method to help you feel stronger, happier, and more confident — in both body and mind. Commit to the method. Repeat it. Sculpt the body you deserve.
